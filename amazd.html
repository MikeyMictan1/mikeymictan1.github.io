AMAZ'D

Prelude Game Studios' premiere project, Amaz'd is a 2D dungeon-crawler roguelike, where you must battle through increasingly more complex mazes, filled with enemies and powerups. You lose your points every second, so be quick, collect coins, and slay monsters to keep it higher! Battle bosses and escape for an amaze-ing score!
